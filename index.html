Frequently asked questions

some of the things you may want to know

What is your photography style?

Our style is spontaneous, natural, and bright. We focus on capturing genuine emotions and candid moments in beautiful, light-filled settings. We aim for timeless images that feel authentic and joyful, rather than overly posed.

Do you deliver every image you shoot?

We do not. We take many photos during an event to ensure we capture the perfect moment. We then carefully curate the best images, removing duplicates, test shots, and any with unflattering expressions. This ensures you receive a polished gallery filled with high-quality, impactful memories.

How long will it take to receive our photos?

Our standard turnaround time for a full event gallery is between 4 to 6 weeks. We believe in taking the necessary time to carefully edit each photo to our high standards. However, we love sharing sneak peeks, so you can expect a few preview images within a week of your event!

What happens if it rains on our event day?

We always come prepared! We can create beautiful, dramatic photos with umbrellas or find a sheltered outdoor location. If the weather is truly impossible, we'll work with you to find the best indoor spots at your venue. Some of the most romantic photos come from embracing the weather!

Do you offer packages, or can we create a custom one?

We offer several popular packages designed for different types of events and coverage needs. However, we understand every event is unique. We are more than happy to work with you to create a custom package tailored specifically to your vision, timeline, and budget.
